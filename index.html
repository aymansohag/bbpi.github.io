Welcome to the Brahmanbaria Polytechnic Institute (BBPI) Website. Brahmanbaria Polytechnic Institute has a unique position among the Polytechnic Institutes, because of its "Politics & Smoking free campus" and modern education system. Commencing the academic programme in the year of 2005, now develop into a centre of excellence in the arena of Diploma Engineering education. This Institute aims to produce technically sound Diploma Engineer as well as capable of thinking in a comprehensive way. We have a strong professional orientation with a focus on academic excellence and students Employability.
